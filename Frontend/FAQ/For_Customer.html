Frequently asked question for Customer

  1. How can I reset my password?
    You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
  2. How do I track my order?
    You can track your order by logging into your account and navigating to the "Order History" section.
  3. What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept credit/debit cards, Netbanking,UPI and COD.
  4. How long does shipping take?
    Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen. Typically, it takes 3-5 business days for standard shipping.
  5. What is your return policy?
    Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our website for detailed information.
  6. How can I contact customer support?
    You can contact our customer support by email at support@Blinkit.com.
  7. Do you offer international shipping?
    No, currently we don't offer shipping internationally.
  8. Can I change or cancel my order after it has been placed?
    Once an order has been placed, changes or cancellations may not be possible. Please contact customer support for assistance.
  9. How do I update my account information?
    You can update your account information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Account Settings" section.
  10. What should I do if I receive a defective product?
    If you receive a defective product, please contact our customer support team immediately for assistance.
