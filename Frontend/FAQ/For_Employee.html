Frequently asked question for Employee

  1. What are the working hours for delievery person?
    Working hours are from 9:00 to 18:00 everyday.
  2. How can I request time off?
    To request time off, employees should fill out the appropriate form available on the employee portal and submit it to their manager for approval.
  3. What training opportunities are available for employees?
    Information about available training opportunities can be found on the employee intranet or by contacting the HR department.
  4. How do I update my personal information?
    Employees can update their personal information, such as contact details and emergency contacts, by accessing the employee portal and navigating to the "Personal Information" section.
  5. How do I access my pay stubs?
    Pay stubs can be accessed through the employee portal under the "Payroll" or "My Pay" section.
  6. What is the process for reporting workplace incidents?
    Employees should report workplace incidents to their immediate supervisor or HR department as soon as possible. Detailed instructions can be found in the employee handbook.
  7. How do I request IT support?
    Employees can request IT support by submitting a ticket through the IT helpdesk system or by contacting the IT department directly.
  8. What is the policy for remote work?
    Information about the remote work policy can be found in the employee handbook or by consulting with HR.
  9. How do I access company documents and resources?
    Company documents and resources can be accessed through the employee portal or shared network drives, depending on the organization's setup.
  10. What should I do if I have a question or concern about company policies?
    Employees should first refer to the employee handbook for information about company policies. If further clarification is needed, they can reach out to HR or their manager for assistance.
